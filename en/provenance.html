Why Separate Assets?

The dual-asset approach creates unprecedented flexibility for collectors, investors, and institutions in the luxury art market.

For Collectors

Liquidity Options

Trade the NFT certificate while keeping the physical piece, or vice versa. Create new liquidity streams without full asset disposal.

Geographic Flexibility

Hold the physical piece in one location while trading the NFT globally. Perfect for international collectors with complex logistics.

For Institutions

Display Programs

Hotels and galleries can hold physical pieces for display while NFT ownership provides marketing value and authenticity verification.

Investment Strategies

Funds can hold NFTs for digital asset exposure while physical custody remains with specialized art storage facilities.
