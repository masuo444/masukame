Fusion of Two Symbols

Masu - Symbol of Prosperity

The "Masu," an ancient Japanese measuring instrument, became the origin of the phrase "masumasu hanei suru" (to prosper more and more). Used to measure rice and sake, it continues to be cherished as a symbol wishing for abundance and business prosperity.


Its square shape represents stability and foundation, while what fills its interior embodies richness itself. It carries the deep-rooted Japanese wish for prosperity that resonates in the hearts of the people.

Turtle - Symbol of Longevity

Known in the saying "cranes live for a thousand years, turtles for ten thousand," the turtle is a sacred creature symbolizing longevity and good fortune. Its gentle and patient nature expresses enduring prosperity and tranquility.


In MASUKAME, the turtle's shell is expressed as the shape of a masu, uniting the symbol of longevity with the wish for prosperity. Through this innovative fusion, it creates new value that transcends mere lucky charms.

Handcrafted by Artisans

Traditional Japanese Craftsmanship

Material Selection

From carefully selected domestic wood, materials with optimal grain and texture are chosen for each piece. The artisan's experienced eye, understanding wood characteristics, identifies the finest materials.

Traditional Techniques

Using Japanese woodworking techniques passed down through generations, each piece is handcrafted individually. Modern tools merge with traditional skills to create precise and beautiful forms.

Finishing

Every step through final finishing is carefully performed by artisan hands. From wood grain-enhancing painting to detailed adjustments, uncompromising dedication to quality is embedded in every piece.

Design Philosophy

MASUKAME's design holds deep meaning beyond mere decoration. It cherishes the Japanese aesthetic concept of "Ma" (negative space), pursuing the beauty of void and harmoniously balanced proportions.


While blending into contemporary lifestyles, its presence brings serenity and dignity to any space. MASUKAME serves as a bridge that brings the joy of ownership along with incorporating Japanese spirituality into daily life.

Heritage for the Modern Era

Tradition and Innovation

MASUKAME preserves the ancient meaning of lucky charms while adding contemporary interpretation. Traditional techniques merge with modern design sensibility to create new cultural value.

It is a timeless art piece that simultaneously expresses respect for the past and hope for the future.

Global Value

While being uniquely Japanese culture, its universal beauty transcends borders for understanding. The wishes for prosperity and longevity are values shared by people worldwide.

MASUKAME also serves as a cultural ambassador conveying the magnificence of Japanese culture to the world.
